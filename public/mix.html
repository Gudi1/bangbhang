The Club





Bang Bhang Presents The 5 Year Bhangra Mixtape Party with Sonnyji & Friends


On Friday 29th June 2012, Bang Bhang exclusively invites you to party it up at what will be the rave of the year! Join BBC Asian Network DJ Sonnyji for a 3 hour set at Birmingham's newest club The Rooftop to celebrate the FIVE Year Bhangra Mixtape party.


Commenting on the upcoming event, Sonnyji said, 'The Saturday night mix show I do on the BBC Asian Network sees me mix & remix Bhangra music with everything from House, Electronica, D&B, Moombahton and so much more. It's this style, sound and musical journey that I want to bring to a club space for my FIVE year party'.


Having played worldwide across India and Dubai, at UK festivals such as Womad and Glastonbury, as well as consistently breaking new ground in his weekly BBC Asian Network Mix Show; Sonnyji is committed to bringing something very special to Birmingham.


The Venue





The Rooftop is the newest nightclub in Birmingham and will be the location of the 5 Year Party.


A main room consisting of a Victorian archway with 'New York Loft' styling, including lit brickwork, an ample dance floor, amphitheater staging, state of the art lighting and a Funktion 1 sound system.


Designed by the people for the people, it keeps in mind the modern day clubber who demands a venue that fuses underground rawness with a twist of glam!


So, all that's left to say now is... See you in the club!
