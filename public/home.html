On Friday 29th June, Bang Bhang exclusively invites you to party it up with BBC Asian Network DJ Sonnyji at Birmingham's newest club The Rooftop to celebrate the FIVE Year Bhangra Mixtape party.

Live in the mix for a minimum three-hour set, Sonnyji will be tearing the house down with quality programming, superb production and the kind of music and mixes meant to inspire, educate and challenge.

Sonnyji will be DJ'ing for 3 hours in the Main Room but joining him will be fellow BBC Asian Network DJ Bobby Friction with a special guest DJ set as well as saXingh on saxophone duties and Del Sondh on live multi percusssion. If all that wasn't enough DEEP CITY SOUL DJ's Matt Mckillop, Owen Taylor & Morais Vibes will be playing the best in House and Funk in the Back Room.

In a UK first Sonnyji brings two styles of forward thinking music into one space with two completely different audiences. Cutting edge Asian & World dance beats in the main room with the most upfront & floor filling House music in the other. With live visuals as well as percussion, saxophone and performers on the night, the atmosphere is set to be an electric carnival that will make you forget your toe from your elbow.

Commenting on the upcoming event, Sonnyji said, 'The Saturday night mix show I do on the BBC Asian Network sees me mix & remix Bhangra music with everything from Hip Hop, House, Disco & D&B. It'os this style, sound and musical journey that I want to bring to a club space for my FIVE year party'.

This night will not only be a celebration of the sound Sonnyji has has created on his weekly radio show but it will also be the launch of his new Club Night, Sound & Movement Bang Bhang. In a nutshell, Bass heavy Dance Beats, House Music and Nu Disco sounds all colliding with the frenetic Beats of Bhangra!

Sonnyji will be performing his new sound at Bestival in the Bollywood Tent later on in the year which means The Mixtape Party will be the perfect place to witness it first.

So what of the venue?

A main room consisting of a Victorian archway with 'New York Loft' styling, including lit brickwork, an ample dance floor, amphitheater staging, state of the art lighting and a Funktion 1 sound system. The Roof Top Club is perfect for the sound you will witness come the 29th June.

Designed by the people for the people, it keeps in mind the modern day clubber who demands a venue that fuses underground rawness with a twist of glam!

All that's left to say now? See you in the club!
