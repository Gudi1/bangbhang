Are You Ready for the Bhang?





Bang Bhang is a new club night, sound and style of performing founded by BBC Asian Network DJ Sonnyji. He's been leading the way these last few years with his BBC Asian Network show the Bhangra Mixtape which sole purpose has been to merge, mix and layer Bhangra sounds into music from the cross genre spectrum be it House, Electronica, Hip Hop, D&B. ...err. . .you get the picture.


Bang Bhang in essence is a whole sound based around his style of DJ'ing which involves looping, cutting and filtering patently eastern sounds with whatâ€™s fresh & exciting in music right now. Think bass heavy, warehouse style dance beats slapped up against upfront South Asian sounds be they percussive Bhangra beats to the funkiest Bollywood cuts and you're nearly there.


Bang Bhang is about taking the percussive elements of Bhangra music, adding a hint of Bolly and slapping those sounds up against Western Dance beats such House Music, Moombahton, NuDisco and Breaks.


It's also about bringing people together from all back rounds who want to experience Bhangra & Asian beats taken into a place not yet ventured musically in the mix. This is not Asian Underground. This is not Bhangra. This is not House. It's just a mix of all these sounds coming together to create one cohesive, fresh and very new rhythm.


In recent years there has not been no outlet for alternative Asian music merging with Western beats. Club nights have kept themselves firmly in the mainstream with a view to not breaking many rules in terms of the music being played. Bang Bhang is here to change all that and offers a sound to those of you who want something more daring to what's on on offer. Expect future Bang Bhang mixes provided by Sonnyji himself right here along with information on all future Bang Bhang club nights and events in the UK or Worldwide.


Bhang Bhang nights will concentrate on quality programming, superb production and the kind of music & mixes that will inspire, educate and challenge. Expect to see live visuals supplied by the in-house VJ's as well as live percussion & saxophone performances played over DJ sets helping create a vibrant, carnival like atmosphere.


Welcome to the new sound. Welcome to Bang Bhang.
