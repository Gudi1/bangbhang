Are You Ready for the Bhang?




Bang Bhang is a club night, sound & style of performing founded by BBC Asian Network DJ Sonnyji. He's been leading the way these last few years with his radio show the Bhangra Mixtape which see's him merge, mix and layer Bhangra sounds into music from the cross genre spectrum be it House, Electronica, Hip Hop, D&B and even Nu Disco.


BANG BHANG IS BASS HEAVY DANCE MUSIC, HOUSE, NU DISCO & D&B COMING TOGETHER WITH BHANGRA BEATS.


The sound is about taking the percussive elements of Bhangra music and re-working the sound into a more progressive way that challenges the listener and elevates the genre into another space.


In recent years there has been no outlet for alternative Asian music merging with Western beats. Bang Bhang offers a sound that is bold, daring, tribal and importantly dance-floor ready. Expect future Bang Bhang mixes provided by Sonnyji himself along with information on all future Bang Bhang events in the UK or Worldwide.


Bhang Bhang nights will concentrate on quality programming, superb production and the kind of Bhangra mixes that will inspire, educate and challenge. Expect to see live visuals supplied by the in-house VJ's as well as live percussion, saxophone and performers.
