THE SUBARU BOXER ENGINE

Few other SUVs drive with the fun and eagerness of Forester, so you won't be surprised by how uniquely it is accomplished. Unlike other more commonly used engine types, the horizontally-opposed configuration of the Subaru Boxer places the pistons 180 degrees apart, for a lower, flatter and more compact profile. This results in the engine, and the overall vehicle, having a lower centre of gravity for better balance. Furthermore, the pistons' opposing movements inherently counteract each other, naturally helping to reduce vibration. This year, a new generation of engines delivers enhanced output, efficiency and emissions, while sharing all the many benefits of the 11 million Subaru Boxer engines already produced.

BETTER BALANCE

Because the engine block has a flatter profile and sits lower in the chassis, the vehicle has a lower centre of gravity, better balance and is more resistant to bodyroll than other engine designs.

SMOOTH OPERATION

Throughout the rev range, the engine smoothly delivers immediate, responsive power. The pistons lay flat and work together in opposition to reduce vibrations, making the ride as comfortable as it is rewarding.

GREATER TRACTION

The engine's flat design is inherently rigid and self-balancing, generating less vibration than most other engines. This delivers greater durability and more reliable performance.

SYMMETRICAL ALL-WHEEL DRIVE

Since introducing Symmetrical AWD in 1972, Subaru has continually advanced its technology to meet the ever-changing needs of our drivers. Unlike most cars a Subaru is engineered from the very start to deliver the traction and stability of AWD, with its engine and drive train in a symmetrical, straight line. Power is delivered to all four wheels simply and directly, for naturally better balance and a natural sense of control. All of which gives you confidence in virtually all conditions and performance that you simply don't expect from an SUV.

DYNAMIC PERFORMANCE

Exceptional traction makes acceleration feel immediate and exhilarating. And the symmetry helps the Forester deliver responsive handling because it's more stable during cornering, for a unique feeling of control at all times.

CONSTANTLY IN CONTACT

The more your tyres are engaged with the road, the more control you have. Power is routed to all four wheels, all the time, in order to maintain the strongest connection to the road as possible, giving you the best handling and control.

ATHLETIC IN AN EMERGENCY

In bad weather, or the precious seconds it takes to react to a sudden situation, the symmetrical drive train with its superb balance provides the stability, agility and traction to help you steer around danger.

SUBARU ENGINEERING

Unlike most cars, the core technology inside every Subaru comes from a perfect idea. We start by creating ideal balance by mating the Subaru Boxer engine to the permanent Symmetrical All Wheel Drive system. This technology then creates the very foundation by which a Subaru excels in all areas.

BALANCE

The layout of the Subaru Boxer engine and Symmetrical AWD naturally balance not just the drivetrains mass but its response as well. Together they deliver power more smoothly and distribute it more effectively to all four wheels, providing the superb grip and balance that defines a Subaru.

CONTROL

The Subaru Boxer engines flat configuration gives the vehicle a lower centre of gravity. Paired with the superior handling of the permanent Symmetrical AWD, this combination provides more precise, responsive and satisfying control in virtually any driving condition.

SAFETY

In poor weather, or in the precious seconds it takes to react to an unexpected situation, the combination of the balanced Subaru BOXER engine and surefooted Symmetrical AWD provide the agility that can help drivers avoid a collision.

SUBARU SAFETY FEATURES

COLLISION AVOIDANCE

Many of the same features that make a Forester fun to drive also contribute to its serious dedication to protecting its occupants from unexpected hazards. The stable, responsive handling of its Symmetrical AWD is enhanced by the low centre of gravity of its Subaru Boxer engine. Its standard Vehicle Dynamics Control, surefooted suspension, generously sized brakes and quick steering equip the driver with excellent evasive abilities. Even the superb visibility of its large glass area is designed to help drivers avoid accidents.*Only Automatic transmission models only.

BRAKING SYSTEM

The standard 4-channel Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) teams with Electronic Brake-Force distribution to redistribute front/rear braking pressure when the vehicle is loaded. Brake Assist provides greater force in sudden braking even if the driver doesn't brake hard enough, soon enough. And Hill Start Assist* holds the brakes for an extra second to help prevent rollback during uphill starts. *Only Manual transmission models.

RING-SHAPED REINFORCEMENT FRAMES

The Ring-Shaped Reinforcement Frames strengthen the passenger cabin from the roof down to the doors, pillars and floor. This design helps to divert and dissipate impact energy away from the occupants in a collision. The front crumple zone is integral to the frame, whilst the cradle mount for the engine and front suspension is designed to help keep these components away from the passenger compartment in a frontal impact.
