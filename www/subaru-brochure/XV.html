THE SUBARU BOXER ENGINE

Few cars in this class deliver the pure spontaneity of Subaru XV. That's thanks to the horizontally - opposed Subaru Boxer engine, which places pistons 180 degrees apart for a lower, flatter profile compared with other layouts. This gives the powerplant - and by extension the whole car - a lower centre of gravity and more balanced weight distribution. On top of that, the pistons' opposing movements counteract each other, which naturally reduces vibration and makes for smoother power delivery

THE SOHC ENGINE

The SOHC engine delivers high fuel efficiency with ultra-clean EURO5-certified emissions. Refinements to its pistons, ports, intake manifold and 70 even its spark plugs help to make it more responsive around town, more satisfying on the highway, and cleaner running on virtually any road. 4

It keeps the engine operating in the most favourable rpm range for any driving speed. with a balance of both power and fuel economy. Specifically tuned for instant response with minimal movement of the accelerator pedal, and a more compact design for greater legroom, lineartronic is the perfect harness for the pure power and broad torque curve of the Subaru BOXER engine.

SUBARU ENGINEERING

Unlike most cars, the core technology inside every Subaru comes from a perfect idea. We start by creating ideal balance by mating the Subaru Boxer engine to the permanent Symmetrical All Wheel Drive system. This technology then creates the very foundation by which a Subaru excels in all areas.

BALANCE

The layout of the Subaru Boxer engine and Symmetrical AWD naturally balance not just the drivetrains mass but its response as well. Together they deliver power more smoothly and distribute it more effectively to all four wheels, providing the superb grip and balance that defines a Subaru.

CONTROL

The Subaru Boxer engines flat configuration gives the vehicle a lower centre of gravity. Paired with the superior handling of the permanent Symmetrical AWD, this combination provides more precise, responsive and satisfying control in virtually any driving condition.

SAFETY

In poor weather, or in the precious seconds it takes to react to an unexpected situation, the combination of the balanced Subaru BOXER engine and surefooted Symmetrical AWD provide the agility that can help drivers avoid a collision.

Subaru Dynamics Control System

SYMMETRICAL ALL-WHEEL DRIVE

Driving satisfaction comes through symmetry and balance. That's why Subaru has been developing and perfecting their Symmetrical AWD system since 1972. Matched with the Subaru Boxer engine for a near-perfect symmetrical layout, it delivers power with exceptional traction, providing balance and stability for total control in ever-changing road conditions.

DYNAMIC PERFORMANCE

Excellent road grip gives acceleration an immediate,exhilarating feel. Symmetry also makes Subaru XV's handling more stable and responsive in cornering, giving a unique 'hands-on' feeling of control in any situation.

CONSTANTLY IN CONTACT

The more your tyres are wedded to the road, the greater control, security and connection you have. Power is permanently routed to all four wheels all the time, making the most of handling and traction.

ATHLETIC IN AN EMERGENCY

In bad weather, or in the precious seconds it takes to react to a sudden situation, the symmetrical drivetrain with almost total lateral balance provides the stability, agility and traction that help you steer round potential danger.

SUBARU SAFETY FEATURES

PASSIVE SAFETY

Driver and passenger protection is a high priority in every element of the Subaru XV – from major features such as seats, to the smaller details like the breakaway pedals. Its energyabsorbing body structure is strengthened with high tensile-strength steel in critical areas, minimising the impact force that can reach the cabin, and increasing bending stiffness. There's also a whole range of advanced interior safety features to help protect occupants directly.

PEDESTRIAN PROTECTION

Made for the urban landscape, the Subaru XV responsibly provides a high level of pedestrian protection. Shock-absorbing space between bonnet and engine helps reduce pedestrian injury if an accident were to happen, as do an energy-absorbing front bumper and specially designed area around the windscreen wipers.

RING-SHAPED REINFORCEMENT FRAMES

These strengthen the passenger cabin from the roof to the doors, pillars and floor. This helps divert and dissipate impact energy away from people in a collision. The frames for the Subaru XV feature high-tensile steel designs that are strong and rigid, yet still lighter in weight.

SUBARU VEHICLE DYNAMICS

UNLIKE MOST CARS, AWD MEANS ALL FOUR WHEELS ARE ALWAYS FULLY ENGAGED

2. SMOOTH HIGHWAY

3. ROUGH ROAD

4. CHANGING WEATHER

The permanent Symmetrical AWD, Vehicle Dynamics Control and precise independent suspension team up to optimise how the tyre responds to the road, to make the most of available traction.
